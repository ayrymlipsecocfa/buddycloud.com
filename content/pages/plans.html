FAQ

How much does it cost?

You can try Buddycloud for free and move on to the best paid plan that gives you the right features and support that you need. Find out your customized solution on our Plans Page. Additionally you can run Buddycloud on your own hardware using the open source code.

What payment methods are accepted?

The hosting platform accepts VISA, Mastercard, Dinersclub and AMEX. Please get in touch if you need an invoice.

What happens if I exceed my plan’s limit?

Don’t worry, you will be notified if this happens so you can decide what to do.
