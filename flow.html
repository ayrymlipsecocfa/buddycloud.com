BC:Flow

Flow, also known as the zone, is the mental state of operation in which a person performing an activity is fully immersed in a feeling of energized focus, full involvement, and enjoyment in the process of the activity.

Designed for You

No loooooonnnnnnng emails! Get it out of your head and move the process forward and get things done.

Everywhere and Anytime

BC:Flow gives you the contextual awareness for that watercooler feeling. Whether it's cat pictures or conversation, you always know what's going on.

Tailored to Your Needs

Conversations are grouped into channels. Open channels to share my ideas with the world. Private channels to share my secrets with my friends.

My Flow to Your Flow

BC:Flow sites interconnect so you can easily work with your friends from other companies running BC:Flow.

Tech specs:

  • Inside the Buddycloud features: secure, scalable communication.
  • Fully federated: BC:Flow sites interconnect using XMPP federation.
  • Totally open source.
  • Mobile friendly.
  • Powerful search: Find when you forget.
  • BC:Flow On-Premises so you can have truly private communication.
  • BC:Flow Hosted versions are available, too.
  • Plugin architecture for IoT.
